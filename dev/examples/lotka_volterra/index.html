Lotka-Volterra

A simple, scalar-valued predator-prey model.

\[\begin{align*} \dd X_t &= (\alpha X_t - \beta X_t Y_t) \dd t + \sigma_1 \dd W^{(1)}_t \\ \dd Y_t &= (\delta X_t Y_t -\gamma Y_t)\dd t + \sigma_2 \dd W^{(2)}_t. \end{align*}\]

Can be called with

@load_diffusion :LotkaVolterra

Auxiliary diffusion

We also provide a linear diffusion that is obtained from linearising SDE above at the equilibrium point. This process can be used as an auxiliary diffusion in the setting of guided proposals. It solves the following stochastic differential equation

\[\begin{align*} \dd \widetilde{X}_t &= \left(- \frac{\beta\gamma}{\delta} \widetilde{Y}_t + \frac{\gamma\alpha}{\delta}\right) \dd t + \sigma_1 \dd W^{(1)}_t \\ \dd \widetilde{Y}_t &= \left(\frac{\alpha\delta}{\beta} \widetilde{X}_t-\frac{\alpha\gamma}{\beta}\right)\dd t + \sigma_2 \dd W^{(2)}_t, \end{align*}\]

and can be called with

@load_diffusion :LotkaVolterraAux
